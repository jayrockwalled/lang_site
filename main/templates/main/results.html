TFIDF

Term Frequency Inverse Document Frequency (TFIDF) is a measure of how important a word is when it appears in a sentence. This is calculated by measuring how often a word is used in the corpus and how often it appears in a document. A similarity score closer to one indicates stronger similarity.

A TFIDF close to 1 indicates the word is very relevant and frequently used in the community.
